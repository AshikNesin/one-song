Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

One Song does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

File Access

When you select a song, the app creates a local copy in its private cache directory on your device. This copy is used solely for playback and is never uploaded or shared. All audio files remain entirely on your device.

App Permissions

One Song requests the following permissions for core functionality only:

  • Storage / Media access — To let you pick an audio file from your device.
  • Notification — To show playback controls in the notification shade (Android 13+).

Internet Access

One Song works entirely offline. No internet connection is required for playback. The app does not make any network requests or communicate with any servers.

Analytics & Tracking

One Song does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs with data collection are included.

Third-Party Services

No third-party services are integrated in One Song.

Children's Privacy

One Song does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here. The "Last updated" date will reflect the revision.

Contact
