## Full Description

**One song. Zero friction.**

Pick a single audio file. Open the app — it plays instantly. Close it — it stops. That's it.

Built for parents who play the same lullaby every single night. No playlists. No libraries. No decisions to make when you're tired.

**What you get**

- **Instant playback** — Opens and auto-plays your song. No taps needed.
- **Loops forever** — Your song repeats until you say stop.
- **Sleep timer** — Set it and forget it. 5 to 60 minutes, your choice.
- **Background audio** — Music keeps playing while locked or in other apps.
- **Notification controls** — Play, pause, or stop from the lock screen.
- **Remembers your song** — Even if you move or rename the original file.

**Perfect for**

- Bedtime lullabies
- White noise loops
- Favorite songs on repeat

No ads. No subscriptions. No accounts. Just one song, always ready.
